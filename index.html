Aftercare Guide

The days after getting your new tattoo are extremely crucial to the healing process and overall outcome of the tattoo, therefore it is imperative to follow the proper guidelines for aftercare. Luckily we're here to provide you with that information!

In order to properly care for your new tattoo, you will need to:

  1. Wash the tattoo and surrounding areas lightly with antibacterial soap, WITHOUT the use of a rag. (Avoid the use of anything abrasive on your new tattoo until the skin is completely healed and feels like your normal skin again!!)
  2. Apply a light layer of AQUAPHOR to the tattoo and rub in thoroughly. Avoid applying too much, a thin layer that works itself into the skin is plenty!
  3. Repeat the processes as needed. Be sure to wash at least once daily and apply AQUAPHOR XX times per day.

Also during the healing process, be sure to avoid:

  • Swimming or prolonged exposure to water. Try to limit water exposure during regular bathing as much as possible.
  • Tanning - Both naturally and with the use of artificial lighting.
  • Any other prolonged exposure to the sun. If sun exposure is unavoidable, be sure to use only approved, tattoo-safe sun blocks!
